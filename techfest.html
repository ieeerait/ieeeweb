Neon Rocket League

Game to be played in 2 teams, each team may contain 1 or 2 contestants depending on the number of people enrolling. Two Remote controlled cars will be placed in the square resembling a football pitch. Their motive is to hit the ball in the centre of the pitch into the goals on either side of the pitch. This is in resemblance to the popular video game "Rocket League". They can move throughtout the pitch to hit the ball, just like a football game. To make this more interesting, before the game, the players have to play a game of darts to choose which car they can pick as theirs according the points they score. The cars will be designed as popular football clubs, from which the players can choose. To top this all off the entire room will be lit in neon lights and the cars too will be neon painted.

Jurassic Mystery

Participants find themselves in a research facility on an isolated island where scientists have been cloning dinosaurs. Unfortunately, the facility has been compromised, and a T-Rex is now on the loose. Participants must navigate through the facility, solve puzzles, and use their wits to escape before the T-Rex finds them.

  1. Stage 1: Players find a researcher's body with clues that contain a passcode
  2. Stage 2: Use the passcode to unlock the PC to crack the code by which they will analyze dinosaur DNA.
  3. Stage 3: The Antidote (Wire buzz round) Discover the antidote for a dinosaur's toxin.
  4. Stage 4: Puzzle to find and destroy a rogue dinosaur egg to prevent more danger.
  5. Stage 5: Final stage: Navigate a maze or series of rooms to Escape Jurassic Park

DARK BYTES

  • Players choose a character and receive a colored life band.
  • Players navigate through a maze, collecting hints and tokens.
  • At the booth, players decipher clues to earn tokens.
  • Players enter an escape room, solving puzzles for additional tokens.
  • Players engage in a code breaker round, decoding challenges for tokens.
  • Using collected tokens, players gain entry to an arcade with PS5 and other games.
  • If a player's life is exhausted, they can choose to continue the game without arcade access or restart.
  • The top arcade scorer receives recognition and prizes.
